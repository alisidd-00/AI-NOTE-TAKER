Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q: How do I create an instant meeting using AI-NOTE GENIUS?
  • A: To create an instant meeting, simply open AI-NOTE GENIUS, go to the 'Create Meetings' section, and click on 'Instant Meeting'.
  • Q: What steps do I need to follow to schedule a meeting in AI-NOTE GENIUS?
  • A: To schedule a meeting, navigate to the 'Schedule Meeting' in the ‘Create Meetings’ section. Fill in the details such as date, time. The app will automatically schedule the meeting.
  • Q: : How can I cancel a meeting I have scheduled in AI-NOTE GENIUS?
  • A: To cancel a scheduled meeting, go to your list of upcoming meetings, select the meeting you wish to cancel, and choose 'Cancel Meeting'.
  • Q: : What is diarization, and how does it work in AI-NOTE GENIUS?
  • A: : Diarization is the process of separating different speakers' voices in a recording. AI-NOTE GENIUS identifies and tags different speakers in the meeting transcript for easier comprehension.
