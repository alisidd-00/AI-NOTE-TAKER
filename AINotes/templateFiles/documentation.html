AI NOTE(GENIUS)

Zoom Integration Guide for AI NOTE GENIUS

Welcome to the comprehensive guide for integrating AI-NOTE GENIUS with Zoom. This guide will walk you through the steps of adding, using, and removing the AI-NOTE GENIUS app to enhance your Zoom experience with advanced note-taking and meeting management capabilities.

Getting Started with Integration

Prerequisites:

  • An active Zoom account.

Installation:

  1. Log in to your Zoom account.
  2. Navigate to the Zoom Marketplace.
  3. Search for AI-NOTE GENIUS and select the app.
  4. Click on ‘Install’ and follow the prompts to integrate AI-NOTE GENIUS with your Zoom account.

Using AI NOTE (GENIUS)with Zoom

Create Meeting:

  1. Open AI-NOTE GENIUS and go to the ‘Create Meeting’ section.
  2. Enter your meeting details and select Zoom as the meeting platform.
  3. Your meeting is now scheduled with Zoom and integrated with AI-NOTE GENIUS for enhanced features like automatic transcription.

Joining and Conducting Meetings:

  1. To join a scheduled Zoom meeting, open AI-NOTE GENIUS and go to your scheduled meetings list.
  2. Click on the relevant meeting.

Transcription and Note-Taking:

  1. After your Zoom meeting ends, go to AI-NOTE GENIUS and click on ‘Meetings’.
  2. Access your transcriptions and notes in the ‘Meetings’ section for review and editing.

Advanced Features:

  • Real-time collaboration: Work on meeting notes with your team in real-time.
  • Keyword alerts: Set up alerts for specific keywords mentioned in your Zoom meetings.

Managing Your Data

Accessing Transcripts:

  1. Go to the 'Meeting' section in AI-NOTE GENIUS
  2. Select a meeting to view its transcript.

Privacy and Security:

  • All data is securely encrypted.
  • Users have full control over their data and its sharing settings.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

  • Issue: Difficulty in integrating AI-NOTE GENIUS with Zoom.

  • Solution: Ensure you have access on Zoom and follow the installation steps closely.

Removing AI NOTE (GENIUS) from Zoom

Uninstallation Process:

  1. Log in to your Zoom account.
  2. Go to ‘My Apps’.
  3. Find AI NOTE (GENIUS) and click ‘Uninstall’.

What Happens to Your Data:

Uninstalling AI NOTE (GENIUS) will not automatically delete your data. You can access your data in AI NOTE (GENIUS) for 30 days post-uninstallation.
